Recipe Finder App

Project Overview

The Recipe Finder app allows users to discover new recipes based on the ingredients they have at home. Users can enter the ingredients they have, and the app will suggest recipes that can be made with those ingredients. The app also allows users to save their favorite recipes, create shopping lists, and follow step-by-step cooking instructions.

Case Study Structure

1. Introduction

Project Title: Recipe Finder

Recipe Finder is a mobile application designed to help users discover new recipes based on the ingredients they have at home. It aims to reduce food waste and make cooking more accessible and enjoyable.

Role and Responsibilities:

- UI/UX Designer

- Responsible for research, design, and usability testing

Project Duration:

2. Problem Statement

Context:

Many people struggle with deciding what to cook based on the ingredients they have. This often leads to food waste or unnecessary trips to the grocery store.

Challenges:

- Users need a simple way to input their ingredients

- The app should provide relevant and diverse recipe suggestions

- Users should be able to save recipes and create shopping lists easily

3. Research

Methods:

- Online surveys to understand user behavior and needs

- Competitive analysis of existing recipe apps

Findings:

- Users want quick and easy recipe suggestions

- Step-by-step instructions with images are preferred

- Saving favorite recipes and creating shopping lists are essential features

Personas:

- **Sarah, 30, Busy Professional:** Needs quick and easy recipes for weeknight dinners

- **Tom, 24, College Student:** Prefers budget-friendly recipes with minimal ingredients
