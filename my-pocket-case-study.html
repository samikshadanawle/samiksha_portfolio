My Pocket App Case Study

Overview

This is a personal project where I designed a My Pocket App Case Study to help users efficiently manage their daily tasks and to-do lists.

Problem Statement

I created a persona representing a busy professional who needs a simple and efficient way to manage their tasks. The user journey map highlighted the process of adding, prioritizing, and completing tasks.

Ideation and Sketching

I brainstormed various design solutions and created initial sketches to explore different layouts and features.
